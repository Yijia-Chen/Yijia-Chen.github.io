Some questions I ponder:

  • How can the climate crisis be structured to major countries (and maybe billionaires?) in a way that overcomes the multi-agent trap?
  • Is neural networks and large models the end state of AI? If not, what’s next?
  • The above question for nuclear risk, AI misalignment, and other important existential issues.
  • Is it ever feasible to safely write to the brain? Enhance human cognition?
  • In the face of human self-destruction from population collapse, how do we achieve higher birth rate while preserving responsible care, healthy childhood experience, proper education, and optionality? What are all available solutions from economic, social engineering, and biological perspectives?
  • What is dark energy? Can intelligence do anything about the eventual “ice death” of the universe?
  • Is it possible to set up a stable multiplanetary societal structure, so that non-instantaneous communication does not lead to Dark Forest hostility?

If you have developed thoughts over any question above, reach out!
